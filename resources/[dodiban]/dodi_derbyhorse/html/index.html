      How a meet runs

      1. 1
        Open the board

        Use /derby to check the open meets, your own record, and the programme charts on each fixture card.

      2. 2
        Player-made courses

        Use /derbyedit to open the route editor: place checkpoints in the world, name your meet, set riders and options, then save. Published routes show under Player races in /derby—anyone can Start race to host a lobby on that course, same as the official programme.

      3. 3
        Choose a fixture

        Under Events, pick the meet you want. See who is hosting, how many riders are already in, and take a quick look at the programme before you join.

      4. 4
        Take your place

        Press Join lobby to put your name down for the field. Then wait with the others until the steward closes the list.

      5. 5
        When they drop the flag

        Once the start is called, follow the route on your map and ride through each checkpoint in order. The red line on the card is the same path you will be riding.

      6. 6
        Across the wire

        First horse home on the published course takes the win. After that, the standings are updated and the room is cleared for the next heat.
