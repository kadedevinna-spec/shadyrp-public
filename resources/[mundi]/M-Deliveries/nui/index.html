Freight Hauler's Handbook

How It Works

Speak with any Delivery Foreman across the frontier to pick up a contract. You'll be given a wagon loaded with cargo and a destination — haul it there safely to collect your pay. Contracts range from simple single-stop runs to multi-stop routes across the territory.

The Job, Step by Step

1 Accept a contract from the Jobs tab
2 Load the packages onto your wagon
3 Follow the route to each drop-off point
4 Unload cargo at the destination and collect payment

A Word on Terrain

Not all roads are created equal, partner. Easier territory like Saint Denis and Annesburg offer smoother rides but lighter pay — the foreman doesn't need to sweeten the deal when the roads do the work. Rougher country like Strawberry and Tumbleweed with their narrow passes and steep trails will test your skill, but the foreman pays better for the trouble. Pick a route that suits your experience — or your ambition.

Reputation

Every successful delivery builds your standing with that foreman. Higher reputation unlocks bigger wagons, multi-stop contracts, and eventually access to more specialized work. Failing a job or losing cargo will cost you standing.

Riding with a Posse

Any driver can form a Delivery Posse and split jobs with their crew. The leader sets each hand's cut of the pay. Everyone earns the same reputation — strength in numbers on dangerous roads.

Wagon Repair

If your wagon takes damage during a delivery, you can repair it on the spot. Look at the wagon and use the Repair Wagon option.

2x Wood Planks and 4x Nails required
Check your wagon's health with Check Wagon Status
Materials are refunded if you cancel the repair

Horse Replacement

If your draft horse dies or becomes detached during a delivery, you can replace it with another horse.

1 Find a wild or untamed horse nearby
2 Lead or lasso it within 15 meters of your wagon
3 Use the Replace Wagon Horse option on the wagon
4 Your cargo transfers automatically to the new wagon
